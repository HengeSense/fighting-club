Classes.

Fighting club offers four different course disciplines: Boxing, Kickboxing, Judo and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. Members are entitled to participate in any classes of their choosing, even from multiple disciplines.

Boxing.

Boxing sessions last 90 minutes and work through a variety of excersizes including technique training, sparring, circuit training, strength excersizes and stretching. Sessions can be rough, but we always enforce safe practices and fairplay.

Sign up

Kickboxing.

Just like our boxing classes, but with kicking! The 90 minute class incorporates conditioning excersizes, technique training and drilling, sparring, circuit training, strength excersizes and stretching.

Judo.

Judo is best known for its throwing techniques, but also involves grappling on the ground using specialized pins, control holds, arm locks and chokes. Our 90 minute class includes conditioning and strength excersizes, technique training, sparring, and stretching.

Sign up

Brazilian Jujitsu.

Brazilian jiu-jitsu is a grappling martial art and self-defense system based on leverage techniques, joint-locks and choke holds. The 90 minute classes include conditioning excersizes, technique training, live sparring, strength excersizes and stretching.

Join.

Joining the fighting club is easy. Simply follow the steps below.

1. Get in touch.

Contact the trainer who leads the course you are interested in and fix a trial appointment.

2. Medical.

Get a medical certificate from your doctor and bring it with you to the free trial appointment.

3. Free Trial.

Enjoy a free session compliments of us. You only need to bring your medical form, light athletic attire and indoor sports shoes.

4. Membership.

If you enjoyed the trial, sign up for a membership. The membership fee of EUR 50 is to be paid at the beginning of each month.
